By playing on MelonSMP, you agree to all rules listed below.

## Gameplay Rules

1. No hacked clients, exploits, or unfair advantages.
2. No ban evasion or alternate accounts to bypass punishment.
3. Max 3 online accounts, 7 total per IP.
4. No griefing, lava casting, or claim bypassing.
5. No trapping or killing defenseless players.
6. No scamming. Loans are at your own risk.
7. No NSFW usernames, skins, or item names.
8. Report bugs and exploits immediately.
9. No solicitation.
10. Follow Minecraft EULA and store terms.

> Rules may change at any time.
